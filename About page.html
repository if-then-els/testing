Our History

Consolata Hospital Kyeni is a faith-based hospital owned by the Catholic Diocese of Embu that was started in 1930 as a dispensary by the Consolata Sisters. The hospital is located in Embu county , embu East District Runyenjes division and Kyeni North location. The Hospita is situated along Nairobi-Meru Highway approximately 32km from Embu Town, on your way to Meru. it is 1.5km off Embu-Meru highway and it borders Kyeni girls high School Kyeni catholic church and sacred heart boarding primary school.
